Adaptive Furigana

Read Japanese on any website at exactly your level.

Adaptive Furigana is a Chrome browser extension for people learning Japanese. Unlike tools that spray furigana over every kanji, it adds readings only to kanji that are harder than your JLPT level. Set your level (N5–N1) and the kanji you already know stay clean, while the ones you don't get a small reading above them.

Features (free)

  • Adaptive furigana — readings appear only above kanji harder than your level (N5–N1)
  • Click-to-define — click any word for its reading, part of speech, and meaning
  • 100% offline — the tokenizer and dictionary are bundled in the extension; no servers, no tracking, no account
  • Per-site control and adjustable ruby size and color
